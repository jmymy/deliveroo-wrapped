Plug in your Deliveroo.

Deliveroo has no public API, so we replay a request captured from the iOS app. Everything stays on your machine.

  1. In Charles, enable SSL Proxying for *.deliveroo.com and trust the Charles cert on your iPhone (full trust in Certificate Trust Settings).
  2. Point the iPhone's Wi-Fi HTTP proxy at your Mac (port 8888), open Deliveroo, and view your Order history.
  3. Right-click the order-history request → Copy cURL Request, and paste it below.

We pull out the Authorization credential and keep every other header verbatim so requests match the real app.
